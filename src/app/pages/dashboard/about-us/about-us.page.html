Welcome to SmintBuster! I am Sergio and I'm the owner of this project. I'm a young programmer who is worried about the environment and reciclying. I studied Software Development and I'm currently working as a QA in a Norwegian company.


This application has been created with an only goal: to facilitate the release of overproduction that companies generte from day to day. In this way, companies will cut their losses and customers and users will get quality products at a reduced price. In addiiton, besides the commitment with people, there is also a commitment with the environment, and ultimately, the Earth, reinforcing a concious and ethic green revolution.
